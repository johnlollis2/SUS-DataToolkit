The System Usability Scale (SUS) is a reliable tool for measuring the usability. It consists of a 10 item questionnaire with five response options for respondents; from Strongly agree to Strongly disagree.

The SUS Analysis Toolkit is a comprehensive tool for analyzing SUS data. It helps you visualize and interpret the results easily.

You can use the toolkit to upload your SUS survey data and get visualizations and interpretations of the SUS scores.

The SUS Analysis Toolkit is an open-source project licensed under the MIT license. Please acknowledge the contributors if you use it.
